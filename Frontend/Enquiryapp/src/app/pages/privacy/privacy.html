Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Information We Collect

When you submit an enquiry or contact form, we collect the following personal information:

  • Name — to address you properly
  • Email address — to respond to your enquiries
  • Phone number — to contact you if needed
  • Message content — the details you share with us

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Responding to your enquiries and service requests
  • Improving our services based on feedback
  • Communicating with you about your enquiries

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined above, or as required by law. Enquiry records are typically retained for 3 years after the last interaction.

Your Rights (GDPR)

Right to Access

Right to Erasure

Right to Data Portability
